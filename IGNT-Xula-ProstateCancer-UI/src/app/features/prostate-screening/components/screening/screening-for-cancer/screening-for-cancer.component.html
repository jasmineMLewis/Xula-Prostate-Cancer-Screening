Screening for Prostate Cancer

Recommendations

Although it is recommended that all men have medical check-ups, wellness or annual visits, you may be surprised to know that there are different opinions about whether men should get screened for prostate cancer or not.

Studies

Several studies have been conducted to evaluate if screening for prostate cancer helps to improve health outcomes and to reduce death.

Results

Based on the results of these studies, medical organizations recommend who should be screened and how often.

PSA Screening Impact Studies

In 2012, two large studies reported different results about the impact of the PSA screening test and found that screening has little impact on reducing death due to prostate cancer.
