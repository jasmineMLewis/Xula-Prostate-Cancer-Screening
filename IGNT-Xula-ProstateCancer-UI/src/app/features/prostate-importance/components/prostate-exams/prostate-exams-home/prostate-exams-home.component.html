Prostate Cancer Screening Exams

Prostate cancer screening exams are done to look for early signs of disease in people who have no symptoms and before they have problems.

The main prostate screening exams are:
  1. Digital Rectal Exam (DRE)
  2. Prostate Specific Antigen (PSA) Test

The DRE and PSA tests can tell if you have prostate problems, but other tests may be needed to see exactly what problem you have.
