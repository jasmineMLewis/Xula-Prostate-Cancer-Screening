Race

What increases your chance of having prostate cancer?

Your Race

  • Prostate cancer is more common in African American men than in White men.
  • The death rate from prostate cancer is also higher for African American men.
  • It is less common in Hispanic, Asian, Pacific Islander, and Native American men than in white men. But they do also get prostate cancer.
  • Medical experts do not yet fully understand the causes of these differences.
