Talk to your Doctor

Men need to understand both the advantages and disadvantages of prostate cancer treatment to make an informed decision about what is best for them.

When choosing among watchful waiting or active treatment, it is important to discuss with your doctor:

  • How advanced your prostate cancer is
  • How healthy you are
  • Your personal life values and expectations
  • The pros and cons of each option you have
  • Other points that matter to you the most
