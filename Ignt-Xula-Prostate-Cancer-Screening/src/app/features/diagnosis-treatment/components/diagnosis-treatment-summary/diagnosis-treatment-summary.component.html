Summary of Module 4

We just learned that:

  • Making a decision about getting prostate cancer screening is only the first step. Depending on the results, you may be later faced with making other decisions about diagnosis and treatment, if needed.
  • There are different tests to confirm the diagnosis of prostate cancer. Although biopsy is the most accurate test we have today, it is not 100% accurate.
  • There are two main options to choose when diagnosed with prostate cancer: watchful waiting and active treatment. Each option has benefits and risks that should be considered.
  • While most prostate tumors NEVER become a serious problem, some tumors MAY cause illness and death.
  • The decision to begin the journey of prostate cancer screening is a decision to make with your doctor!
