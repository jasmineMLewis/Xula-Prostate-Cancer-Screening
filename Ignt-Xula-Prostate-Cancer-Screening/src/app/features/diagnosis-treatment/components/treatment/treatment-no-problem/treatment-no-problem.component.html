Tumors that Grow Slowly

Most prostate tumors NEVER become a serious problem

  • Most tumors grow slowly. They usually don’t cause any symptoms during a man’s life and the man dies of something else.
  • Autopsies on men who have died from causes other than prostate cancer have shown that many men actually had prostate cancer and did not know it. For these men, prostate cancer had never become bothersome enough to be diagnosed.
Tumor

Tumor: A mass of tissue formed when cells in the body do not grow normally. Tumors can be malignant (cancerous) or benign (not cancerous).
