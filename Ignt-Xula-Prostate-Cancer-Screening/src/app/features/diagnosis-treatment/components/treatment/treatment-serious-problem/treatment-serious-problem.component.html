Tumors that Grow Fast

Some prostate tumors DO become a serious problem

  • Some tumors can grow quickly.
  • They can spread beyond the prostate.
  • They can cause illness and death.
Tumor

Tumor: A mass of tissue formed when cells in the body do not grow normally. Tumors can be malignant (cancerous) or benign (not cancerous).
