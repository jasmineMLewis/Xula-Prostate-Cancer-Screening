New Blood & Urine Tests

There are two new screening tests that may be recommended to men with high PSA values

  • The Prostate Health Index (PHI) is a blood test that may detect if you have an aggressive type of cancer that may cause problems.
  • The PCA3 Urine Test measures the amount of PCA3 present, a gene that is only in the prostate.

Higher values of PHI and PCA3 indicate a greater chance that you have prostate cancer.
