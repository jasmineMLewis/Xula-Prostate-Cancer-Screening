What to Know about Prostate Cancer?
Prostate cancer:
  • Begins when cells in the prostate gland start to grow out of control.
  • Prostate cancer may grow very slowly.
  • Many men with prostate cancer die of other causes, and many never know they have it.
  • Can become a very serious and fatal disease, if not discovered early.
  • Is the second leading cause of cancer-related deaths among men. Almost 30,000 men die each year from prostate cancer in the U.S.
