Inflammation

Prostatitis

This problem is called prostatitis, which means inflammation of the prostate.

Prostatitis is usually due to an infection and can be treated with antibiotics or anti-inflammatory medications.

Prostatitis is not prostate cancer.

Antibiotic & Anti-Inflammatory

Antibiotic: A medicine (such as penicillin or its derivatives) that inhibits the growth of or destroys microorganisms

Anti-inflammatory drugs: Medicine that helps reduce inflammation, which often helps to relieve pain.
