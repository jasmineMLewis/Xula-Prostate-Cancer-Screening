Enlarged Prostate

Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia

This problem is also called benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH).

As men age, the prostate tends to increase in size. As the prostate grows it can cause the urethra to narrow and decrease urine flow.

BPH is not prostate cancer.

BPH is treated with medications.

Urethra

Urethra: The tube that extends from the bladder to the tip of the penis. It carries urine from the bladder and, during ejaculation, semen, from the prostate gland, out through the penis.
