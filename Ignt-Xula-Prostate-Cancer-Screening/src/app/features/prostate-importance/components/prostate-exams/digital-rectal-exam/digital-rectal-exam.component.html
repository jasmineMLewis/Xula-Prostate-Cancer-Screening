DRE (the finger exam)

DRE stands for Digital Rectal Exam.
  • It is a quick exam to check the prostate for hard or lumpy areas.
  • For this test, a doctor inserts a gloved and lubricated finger into the rectum. This allows the doctor to feel the back portion of the prostate.
  • Your doctor will feel its size and search for any irregular or abnormally firm areas.
  • The DRE is a brief procedure which can cause some discomfort.
