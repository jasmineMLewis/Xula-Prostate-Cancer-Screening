Symptoms of Prostate Problems

You should see your doctor if you have any of these symptoms:
Urine Related Symptoms
A weak urine stream Blood in your urine Dribbling at the end of urination
Trouble starting or keeping a urine stream Unable to delay urination Unable to empty your bladder completely
Urinating eight or more times a day Urine that has an unusual color or smell Waking often to urinate when you sleep
