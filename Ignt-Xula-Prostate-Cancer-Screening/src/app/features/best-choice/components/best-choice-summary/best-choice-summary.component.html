Summary of Module 5

We just learned that:

  • Regardless of your prostate cancer risks, as a man you will be faced with making the decision about screening for prostate cancer.
  • There is no right or wrong decision regardless of if you choose or not to be screened.
  • The best choice you can make today is to learn, ask questions, and discuss these issues with your doctor and with the people important in your life.
  • The decision to begin the journey of prostate cancer screening is yours to make!
