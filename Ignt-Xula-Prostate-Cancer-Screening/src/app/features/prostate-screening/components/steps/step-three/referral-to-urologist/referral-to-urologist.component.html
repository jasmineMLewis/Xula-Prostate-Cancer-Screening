What if the screening results are abnormal?

If you receive a second PSA test and your PSA level is still high, your doctor may refer you to a urologist.

The urologist will:
  • Review your symptoms, medical and family history, and order more tests, as needed
  • Give a more accurate diagnosis about what is going on with your prostate
  • If necessary, discuss different treatment options with you
Urologist

Urologist A doctor (surgeon) who specializes in disorders of the urinary system and the male reproductive system.
