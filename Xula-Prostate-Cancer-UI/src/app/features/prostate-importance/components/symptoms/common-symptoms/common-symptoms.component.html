Other Common Symptoms

You should see your doctor if you have any of these symptons:
Symptons
Fever, chills, or body aches
Great discomfort or pain in your belly
Pain in the area between your scrotum and anus

Pain in your penis or scrotum
Pain while urinating or after ejaculation
Your Doctor
Your doctor will evaluate your symptoms and conduct a physical exam. Your doctor may also order specific tests to confirm if you have a prostate problem.
