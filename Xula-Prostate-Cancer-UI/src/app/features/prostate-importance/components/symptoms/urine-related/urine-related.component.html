Urine Related Symptons

You should see your doctor if you have any of these symptons:
Symptons
A weak urine stream
Urine that has an unsusal color or smell
Dribbling at the end of urination
Symptons
Trouble starting or keeping a urine stream
Blood in your urine
Unable to empty your bladder completely
Symptons
Urinating eight or more times a day
Unable to delay urination
Walking often to urinate when you sleep
