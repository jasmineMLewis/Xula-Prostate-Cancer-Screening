Prostate Specific Antigen

PSA stands for Prostate Specific Antigen.
  • PSA is a substance (protein) made by the prostate gland. The prostate gland releases the PSA into the blood.
  • The PSA test measures the level of PSA in the blood.
  • As a rule, the higher the PSA level in the blood, the more likely it is that a prostate problem is present.
  • This is not always the case, which is why a high PSA level needs to be followed by additional tests.
