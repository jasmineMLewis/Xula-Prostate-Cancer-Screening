What is the Prostate?

Prostate

The prostate is a gland in men that makes the fluid that carries sperm.


Location

It is located in front of the rectum just below the bladder.


Size

It is about the size of a walnut.

Bladder & Rectum

Bladder: The organ that stores urine.

Rectum: The lower part (last 8-10 inches) of the large intenstine. The rectum stores solid waste (stool) until it leaves the body through the anus.
