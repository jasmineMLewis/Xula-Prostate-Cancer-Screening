If test results are abnormal?

If you have had several prostate cancer screening tests (PSA and/or DRE) with abnormal results, it does NOT mean that you have prostate cancer.

The first step is to discuss with your doctor your results and personal conditions.

You may be referred to a urologist to discuss the benefits and risks of the options you have. The urologist may order additional testing procedures.

Let’s look at each one of these options in detail...

Urologist

Urologist: A doctor (surgeon) who specializes in disorders of the urinary system and the male reproductive system.*

* See Module 3, “Screening”, for additional information.
