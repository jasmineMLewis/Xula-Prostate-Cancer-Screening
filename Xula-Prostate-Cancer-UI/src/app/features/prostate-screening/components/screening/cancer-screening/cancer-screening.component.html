What is Cancer Screening?

Before Any Symptoms
Cancer screening is when a doctor looks for cancer before a person has any symptoms.
Early Stage
Cancer screening can help find cancer at an early stage.
  • When cancer is found early, it may be easier to treat.
  • By the time symptoms appear, cancer may have begun to spread.
