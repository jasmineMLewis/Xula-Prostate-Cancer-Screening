Should I get a PSA test?

The best recommendation is that you take an active role when making the decision, instead of asking the doctor to make the decision for you.

Current guidelines recommend Shared Decision Making, meaning:
  • You talk with your doctor about the general risks and benefits of the PSA test for prostate cancer screening, and
  • Together, you and your doctor, make a decision about the choice that will be best for you as an individual.
