Recommendations About PSA Screening

Research Studies

Based on the research studies, medical organizations recommend that men discuss the pros and cons of PSA screening with their healthcare providers.

There is not full agreement about who should have the PSA test or when to begin screening.

Recommendations

  • In general, most organizations agree that men 50-69 years old should have these conversations with their providers.
  • However, for men in high risk groups (men with family history of prostate cancer and those of African American descent) it is recommended to begin screening earlier.
